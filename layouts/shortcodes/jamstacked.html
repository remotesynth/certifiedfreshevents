What is Jamstacked?

Jamstacked was published from March 2020 until July 2024. It focused on the Jamstack philosophy and ecosystem for web development. Jamstack was an evolving term originally created by Matt Biilmann, founder and CEO of Netlify, in 2015. It initially focused on static web site development enhanced with dynamic JavaScript, but eventually encompassed the entire ecosystem of front-end web frameworks including tools like Next.js and Astro.

Over 4+ years, Jamstacked monitored these changes, new frameworks, new tooling, new platforms and more. You are invited to review our archive if you would like to get a sense of what went on in the world of full-stack web development during those years.
